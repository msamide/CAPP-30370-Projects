Goals/Plans After Graduation

Personally, I want live in a place where I can happily call home. This is meant to very vague since I really don't know where I want to live yet. I was born and raised in the Midwest and have always loved the people and culture of the Midwest, but I have never liked the weather. So I could see myself living in the Midwest, one of the Carolinas, or Florida. Hopefully soon after or during college, I will be able to find a woman, willing to marry me, settle down, and start a family.

Professionally, I hope to be working at the intersection of business and technology. Both have always interested me and I know that they are going to be growing sectors in the coming years. It's possible I could work for a large corporation, a small startup, or forge my own path and start my own business. I love the independence, freedom and accmoplishment that comes with entrepreneurship so I am particularly drawn towards that.
